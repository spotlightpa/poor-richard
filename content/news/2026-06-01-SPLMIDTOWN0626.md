Spotlight PA is teaming up with Midtown Scholar Bookstore in Harrisburg for &#34;Matter of Fact,&#34; a new quarterly series of community conversations, panels, and presentations exploring the most pressing issues shaping our commonwealth — and the people working to make it stronger.

The series kicks off<strong> Tuesday, June 9, from 6–7 p.m.</strong> with &#34;The Local News Crossroads: How We Got Here — and Where We Go Next.&#34; All <a href="https://www.simpletix.com/e/spotlight-pa-at-the-scholar-matter-of-fact-tickets-272697#smtx-click"><strong>tickets are free and open to the public</strong></a><strong>.</strong>

There are more forces than ever working to silence free and fearless reporting, and fewer resources than ever to support the independent, investigative journalism that holds government accountable to &#34;We the People.&#34; Join Spotlight PA CEO &amp; President <strong>Christopher Baxter</strong> and <strong>David Boardman</strong> — who led the Seattle Times to four Pulitzer Prizes before becoming dean of the Klein College of Media and Communication at Temple University — for a conversation on why a free society and a free press are inextricably linked. An open audience Q&amp;A will follow.

🎟️ <a href="https://www.simpletix.com/e/spotlight-pa-at-the-scholar-matter-of-fact-tickets-272697#smtx-click"><strong>Get tickets here</strong></a><strong></strong>

At Spotlight PA, our work is more than writing stories — it&#39;s about being a positive force in our communities for discussion, learning, and change for the better. &#34;Matter of Fact&#34; brings that mission to life, creating space for people in the Harrisburg area to come together, ask questions, and engage with the issues that matter most.
