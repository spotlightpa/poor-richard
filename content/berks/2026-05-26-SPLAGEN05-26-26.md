READING — Here&#39;s what Berks County local governments are up to this week.

Note that agendas may not be available until 24 hours before a meeting. This week, Memorial Day has shifted many meetings.

## Tuesday, May 26

🔨 Reading City Council will consider <a href="https://www.readingpa.gov/calendar/regular-council-meeting-109">allowing adaptive reuse in two zoning districts</a> and adding underground parking in the transit-oriented development overlay district.

👵 Reading Planning Commission will review plans to consolidate 10 parcels and reuse a blighted industrial building at 201 S. 6th St. to <a href="https://www.readingpa.gov/images/planningcomm/City_of_Reading_Planning_Commission_Agenda_-_5.26.26.pdf">create a 46-unit apartment building</a> for older people and discuss Hampden Boulevard traffic calming.

🏢 Cumru Township Zoning Hearing Board will hold a hearing for a request from DS Property Ventures to <a href="https://www.cumrutownship.com/">build four apartment buildings</a> with 28 units at 946 and 970 Morgantown Road.

🚓 Hamburg Borough Council will discuss <a href="https://hamburgboro.com/">allowing police to make arrests without warrants</a> for certain summary offenses, limiting the number of allowed livestock, and closing Franklin Street between North 3rd and Apple streets to vehicles.
