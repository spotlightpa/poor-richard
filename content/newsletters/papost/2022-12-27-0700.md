day</a>.<br/>\n<br/>\nThey also <a href=\"https://www.spotlightpa.org/news/2022/07/pa-budget-education-funding-stimulus-money-plan/\" style=\"mso-line-height-rule: exactly;-ms-text-size-adjust:
