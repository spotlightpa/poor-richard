There are also critics who say states like New Jersey — which expects to collect $30 million in cannabis revenue this year — <a href=\"https://www.nj.com/marijuana/2022/01/nj-cannabis-coalition-says-murphy-state-should-improve-expungement-process.html\" target=\"_blank\" style=\"mso-line-height-rule: exactly;-ms-text-size-adjust: 100%;-webkit-text-size-adjust: 100%;color: #5d9eca;font-weight: bold;text-decoration: underline;\">aren&#39;t doing enough</a> to address the lingering effects caused by decades of criminalization.<br/>\n<br/>\nIf you’re planning to make the trip east, there are a few things you’ll need to know to avoid a <a href=\"https://www.mcall.com/news/police/mc-nws-lehigh-valley-police-on-new-jersey-recreational-marijuana-20220420-ll2rq6buubczrc7j27jnyhtphq-story.html\" target=\"_blank\" style=\"mso-line-height-rule: exactly;-ms-text-size-adjust: 100%;-webkit-text-size-adjust: 100%;color: #5d9eca;font-weight: bold;text-decoration: underline;\">cannabis-related arrest</a> of your own. <br/>\n<br/>\nAnd if you haven’t had cannabis since high school, there’s a few more things you should know to avoid getting excruciatingly high.<br/>\n<br/>\nHere&#39;s how it works: <br/>\n<br/>\n<strong>Who can buy it? </strong><br/>\n<br/>\nLike alcohol, you have to be at least 21 years old with an ID, and that’s pretty much it.<br/>\n<br/>\n<strong>How much can I buy? </strong><br/>\n<br/>\nUp to one ounce of dried flower (the form most people are familiar with); up to five grams of concentrates, resins, or oils (the latter often consumed via vape pens); and up to 1,000 milligrams of edibles. Note: These are limits, not recommended amounts. Most people will require much less.<br/>\n<br/>\n<strong>Where can I buy it? </strong><br/>\n<br/>\n<a href=\"https://data.nj.gov/Reference-Data/New-Jersey-Cannabis-Dispensary-Locations/uyq5-2c2g\" style=\"mso-line-height-rule: exactly;-ms-text-size-adjust: 100%;-webkit-text-size-adjust: 100%;color: #5d9eca;font-weight: bold;text-decoration: underline;\">Here’s a map</a> of the dozen or so dispensaries currently cleared for recreational sales. As of Thursday, some were doing sales by appointment only, so maybe call ahead. <br/>\n<br/>\n<strong>How much does it cost?</strong><br/>\n<br/>\nPrices vary by location and product. Let this <a href=\"https://risecannabis.com/dispensary-menu/new-jersey/paterson-adult-use-rec-menu/\" style=\"mso-line-height-rule: exactly;-ms-text-size-adjust: 100%;-webkit-text-size-adjust: 100%;color: #5d9eca;font-weight: bold;text-decoration: underline;\">recreational menu</a> from a dispensary in Paterson be a guide. Cannabis is still federally illegal, meaning most credit card companies won’t touch the proceeds. While debit cards might work at New Jersey dispensaries, cash is the safest bet.<br/>\n<br/>\n<strong>Am I allowed to bring legally purchased cannabis from New Jersey into Pennsylvania? </strong><br/>\n<br/>\nDefinitely not. That’s illegal under Pennsylvania law and federal law for everyone: Pennsylvanians, Pennsylvania medical marijuana cardholders, and New Jerseyans, too.<br/>\n<br/>\n<strong>Where can I consume it? </strong><br/>\n<br/>\nOnly on private property in New Jersey with the property owner’s permission. Also don’t consume there and then drive back to Pennsylvania. That’s a DUI in both states. (Seriously, don’t consume and drive at all.)<br/>\n<br/>\n<strong>How much should I consume? </strong><br/>\n<br/>\nThe answer depends almost entirely on your experience with and tolerance for THC, pot’s main psychoactive ingredient. <br/>\n<br/>\nIf you’re inhaling it for the first time in a while or ever, noted enthusiast Seth Rogen suggests starting with a small sip and <a href=\"https://twitter.com/Sethrogen/status/1387175801473302528?s=20&amp;t=ZL1cyEezNt4cYe4ixifeDw\" target=\"_blank\" style=\"mso-line-height-rule: exactly;-ms-text-size-adjust: 100%;-webkit-text-size-adjust: 100%;color: #5d9eca;font-weight: bold;text-decoration: underline;\">waiting 15 minutes</a>.<br/>\n<br/>\nIf you’re ingesting it for the first time in a while or ever, cannabis entrepreneur Annie Nelson — wife to Willie — suggests <a href=\"https://www.thedailybeast.com/microdosing-weed-how-to-find-the-right-serving-size-of-edible-cannabis\" target=\"_blank\" style=\"mso-line-height-rule: exactly;-ms-text-size-adjust: 100%;-webkit-text-size-adjust: 100%;color: #5d9eca;font-weight: bold;text-decoration: underline;\">a small dose</a> of between 2.5 milligrams and 5 milligrams and waiting up to two hours.
