&#34;<a href=\"https://www.nytimes.com/2007/03/30/realestate/greathomes/30havens.html\" target=\"_blank\" style=\"mso-line-height-rule: exactly;-ms-text-size-adjust: 100%;-webkit-text-size-adjust:
